# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 13, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to कविता व्यासपीठ (Poetry Platform). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our multilingual poetry platform.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Username, email address, display name, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Bio, preferred language, profile picture (optional)
- **Content:** Poems, comments, ratings you submit
- **Payment Information:** Transaction details for premium subscriptions (processed securely through Razorpay)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, time spent, features used
- **Device Information:** Browser type, device type, IP address
- **Cookies:** Session cookies for login, preference cookies for theme/language settings
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics data for improving user experience

### 3. Third-Party Data
- **Google AdSense:** Advertising cookies and tracking for ad personalization
- **Payment Processor (Razorpay):** Payment transaction data

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Account Management:** Create and maintain your account
2. **Content Delivery:** Display your poems, manage your submissions
3. **Communication:** Send important updates, respond to inquiries
4. **Payment Processing:** Handle premium subscriptions and featured poet payments
5. **Platform Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, add features
6. **Legal Compliance:** Meet legal obligations, prevent fraud

### Advertising
- We use Google AdSense to display ads
- Ads may be personalized based on your browsing behavior
- You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your browser settings

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:
- **Contract:** To provide services you've signed up for
- **Consent:** For optional features like analytics and personalized ads
- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our platform and prevent abuse
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable laws

## Cookies and Tracking

### Types of Cookies We Use
1. **Essential Cookies:** Required for login, session management
2. **Preference Cookies:** Remember your theme (light/dark), language choice
3. **Analytics Cookies:** Google Analytics for usage statistics
4. **Advertising Cookies:** Google AdSense for ad personalization

### Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through:
- Browser settings (disable/delete cookies)
- Google Ad Settings (opt-out of personalized ads)
- "Do Not Track" browser setting (we honor this)

### Rights You Have
1. **Access:** Request a copy of your data
2. **Correction:** Update incorrect information
3. **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
4. **Portability:** Export your data in machine-readable format
5. **Object:** Opt-out of certain data processing
6. **Restrict:** Limit how we use your data
7. **Withdraw Consent:** Stop optional data processing

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Data

- **Deleted Accounts:** 30-day grace period, then permanent deletion
